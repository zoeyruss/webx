'PIXELS'

by Tony Kim


A pixel within a vast universe.

Not knowing what to do, wanders around aimlessly.

Worrying about its importance, its placement, its purpose, within this cruel big bad world.

the pixel felt assured knowing that the pixel is not alone.

Because without the other pixels, the single block of pixel is nothing.

The world becomes full when all the pixels gather together to create a complete picture.

Our goals, our values, our beauty, our people, us, you, and me,

Extend out of the two-dimensional plane and into infinity

Because together, We can share eternal love and wholesomeness to each other to help create a beautiful world.

Together.
